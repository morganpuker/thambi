Chicken Sopas (Filipino chicken soup)

Description

Ingredients 4 servings 3 cloves garlic 1 medium chopped onions 1 lb chicken 2 cups cabbage 1 small carrot, diced Salt and pepper 1 cup elbow macaroni

Cooking

1

Boil chicken until cooked. When cooked, shred chicken meat and reserve chicken stock. Sauté onions and garlic until soft

2

Add carrots and macaroni and the chicken stock. Bring to a boil. When the macaroni is 75% cooked add the shredded chicken and simmer for 5 mins.

3

Put the cabbage and simmer for 5 more minutes Add salt and pepper to taste
